WONKA

Based on the extraordinary character at the center of Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, "Wonka" tells the wonderous story of how the world's greatest inventor, magician and chocolate-maker became the beloved Willy Wonka we know today.

The story of how he went from a young adult selling chocolate in a small candy store to an eccentric genius known all over the world.

PG ADVENTURE FAMILY COMEDY MUSICAL • 1h 56m

Cast Timothée Chalamet, Rowan Atkinson, Calah Lane, Sally Hawkins, Keegan-Michael Key, Olivia Colman, Paterson Joseph, Matt Lucas, Hugh Grant and Jim Carter
